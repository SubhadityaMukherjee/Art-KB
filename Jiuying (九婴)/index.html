Jiuying (九婴)

  • According to Huainanzi, Jiuying was a deadly Chinese
    mythical creature of fire and water, with nine heads and a cry like that of
    a wailing baby. Extremely hostile to mankind, it was ultimately killed by
    Hou Yi.
