# N端wa
- The mother goddess of ancient Chinese beliefs, N端wa was the sister and wife of Fuxi. Her most famous myth is that of her repairing a damaged pillar of heaven with a five-colored stone. N端wa also cameoed in _Investiture of the Gods_ as the goddess who laid the cornerstone for the Shang-Zhou conflict.
