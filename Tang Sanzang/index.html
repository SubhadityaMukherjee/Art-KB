Tang Sanzang

  • More famously known as Tripitaka to the Western World, Tang Sanzang was based on Xuan Zang, a real-life Tang Dynasty Monk who went on a pilgrimage to India to collect Buddhist sutras. In Journey to the West, he was Sun Wukong’s second master. He was also consistently portrayed by author Wu Cheng’en as naïve, hapless, and excessively benevolent.
