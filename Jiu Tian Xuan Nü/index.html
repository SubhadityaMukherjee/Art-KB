Jiu Tian Xuan Nü

  • The “Mysterious Maiden of the Nine Heavens” is an ancient Chinese goddess described as the teacher of Huang Di. In this role, she was also the advisor who assisted him during the epic confrontation with Chi You. While usually portrayed as a stunningly beautiful woman in Chinese movies nowadays, her original form was that of a human-headed bird。
